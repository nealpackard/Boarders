Your Table. Your Board. Your Rules.

We are tearing down the boundaries of traditional dining. No rigid plates, no compromises.

Look closely at the world’s greatest street foods and you’ll find a universal truth: every culture has perfected the art of crossing borders using a warm flatbread, a stone-pressed tortilla, or a crusty baguette to hold their finest seasoned fillings.

Crossing Boards brings these global traditions straight to your table on sprawling, custom-milled boards. Everything arrives completely deconstructed—leaving the final voyage entirely up to you.

Crossing Borders. One Bite at a Time.

The greatest culinary traditions don't stay behind lines. Across every border, the world’s most iconic cuisines share a common language: an incredible fire-baked flatbread, a fresh tortilla, or an artisanal loaf layered with rich, savory fillings and vibrant toppings.

We serve the world’s ultimate comfort foods completely deconstructed on premium wooden boards. With oven-warm breads, hot mains, and house-made sauces at your fingertips, you orchestrate the journey.

CONFIDENTIAL PARTNER BRIEF

1. Executive Summary

Crossing Boards is an upscale, experience-driven casual concept capitalizing on the demand for high-theatric beverage attachment and structural veto-vote mitigation. Shifting final assembly to the table slashes BOH headcount and drastically reduces ticket times.

2. Operations Strategy

To combat real-world table crowding, the floor configuration incorporates custom vertical iron board towers. Hot components are insulated inside flush wooden well inserts holding line-heated cast iron or volcanic soapstones, preserving safe handling and ideal temperature mapping.
