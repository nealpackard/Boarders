CONFIDENTIAL PARTNER BRIEF

1. Executive Summary

Boarders is an innovative, upscale-casual restaurant concept capitalizing on the market demand for experiential dining and high-margin craft beverage programs. By shifting final assembly mechanics to the guest, the kitchen layout optimizes line efficiency, slashes ticket times, and lowers required back-of-house labor expenses.

2. Menu Management: The 70/30 Architecture

To insulate the supply chain while keeping consumer engagement high, Boarders maps operations to a 70/30 format:

  • The Anchors (70%): Permanent global concepts maximizing prep predictability and high profit margins.
  • The Sails (30%): Seasonal menu rotations capturing high-volume culinary trends every 90 days.

3. Dietary Inclusivity & Veto-Vote Capitalization

Group booking decisions are heavily dictated by dietary restrictions. Boarders designs premium, standalone Vegan, Vegetarian, and Gluten-Free (GF) destinations directly into the menu layout rather than handling them as an afterthought. Because elements are delivered completely deconstructed into isolated board wells, cross-contamination risks are mitigated seamlessly at the line level.

4. High-Margin Beverage Attach Strategies

Beverage programs maintain the strongest profit yields in the industry. Boarders drives average check values up by directly embedding curated, high-theatric craft cocktail pairings underneath each food menu item, removing friction from the upsell process.

5. Operational Hurdles Mitigated

  • Table Space Constraints: Custom dining floor footprints will leverage custom-milled vertical board towers, saving essential table real estate.
  • Thermal Retention: Wooden layout frames will hold flush, custom-insulated wells engineered to house pre-heated cast-iron inserts straight from the line, keeping main features hot without wilting garnishes.
