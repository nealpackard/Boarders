Your Table. Your Board. Your Rules.

We are tearing down the boundaries of traditional dining. No rigid plates, no compromises.

Look closely at the world’s greatest street foods and you’ll find a universal truth: every culture has perfected the art of crossing borders using a warm flatbread, a stone-pressed tortilla, or a crusty baguette to hold their finest seasoned fillings.

Crossing Boards brings these global traditions straight to your table on sprawling, custom-milled boards. Everything arrives completely deconstructed—leaving the final voyage entirely up to you.

Crossing Borders. One Bite at a Time.

The greatest culinary traditions don't stay behind lines. Across every border, the world’s most iconic cuisines share a common language: an incredible fire-baked flatbread, a fresh tortilla, or an artisanal loaf layered with rich, savory fillings and vibrant toppings.

We serve the world’s ultimate comfort foods completely deconstructed on premium wooden boards. With oven-warm breads, hot mains, and house-made sauces at your fingertips, you orchestrate the journey.

CONFIDENTIAL PARTNER BRIEF

1. Executive Summary

Crossing Boards is an innovative, experience-driven casual concept capitalizing on the surging market demand for high-theatric beverage attachment and structural veto-vote mitigation. Shifting the final assembly of complex street foods cleanly to the guest's table layout optimizes kitchen prep pipelines, slashes ticket times, maximizes BOH line efficiency, and directly lowers overall labor expenses.

2. Menu Management: The 70/30 Architecture

To completely insulate the culinary supply chain from commodity volatility while keeping consumer engagement and repeat traffic exceptionally high, Crossing Boards maps food and beverage operations to a strict 70/30 architecture format:

  • The Anchors (70%): Permanent global crossing concepts maximizing core item predictability, bulk prep speed, and high profit margins.
  • The Sails (30%): Seasonal menu rotations capturing high-volume culinary trends, limited-time neighborhood features, and fast-moving regional explorations every 90 days.

3. Dietary Inclusivity & Veto-Vote Capitalization

Group booking decisions are heavily dictated by individual dietary restrictions. Crossing Boards designs premium, standalone Vegan, Vegetarian, and Gluten-Free (GF) destinations directly into the menu framework rather than handling them as an afterthought. Because food elements are delivered completely deconstructed into isolated, beautiful board wells, cross-contamination risks are mitigated seamlessly at the line level, ensuring maximum group consensus safety.

4. High-Margin Beverage Attach Strategies

Beverage programs maintain the strongest profit yields in the hospitality industry. Crossing Boards actively drives average check values up by directly embedding curated, high-theatric craft cocktail pairings and interactive multi-person large format punch kits underneath each core menu item, completely removing friction from the natural table upselling process.

5. Operational Hurdles Mitigated

  • Table Space Constraints: Custom casual footprints will leverage engineered, vertical iron board towers, tiering accoutrement wells upward to preserve table real estate and enhance visual presentation.
  • Thermal Retention: Wooden layout frames will hold custom-fitted, insulated wells engineered to house pre-heated volcanic soapstones or cast-iron trenchers straight from the line, keeping main components hot without melting fresh garnishes.
