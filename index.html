Your Table. Your Board. Your Rules.

We are tearing down the boundaries of traditional dining. No rigid plates, no culinary compromises.

Look closely at the world’s greatest street foods and you’ll find a universal truth: every culture, from the Mediterranean to the Americas, has perfected the art of crossing borders using a warm flatbread, a stone-pressed tortilla, or a crusty baguette to hold their finest seasoned fillings.

Boarders brings these global traditions straight to your table on sprawling, custom-milled boards. Everything arrives completely deconstructed alongside vibrant, regional accoutrements—leaving the final voyage entirely up to you.

Crossing Borders. One Bite at a Time.

The greatest culinary traditions don't stay behind lines. Across every border, the world’s most iconic cuisines share a common language: an incredible fire-baked flatbread, a fresh tortilla, or an artisanal loaf layered with rich, savory fillings and vibrant toppings.

Boarders captures this global phenomenon on a single table. We serve the world’s ultimate comfort foods completely deconstructed on premium wooden boards. With oven-warm breads, hot mains, and house-made sauces at your fingertips, you orchestrate the journey.

3. Dietary Inclusivity & Veto-Vote Capitalization

Group booking decisions are heavily dictated by dietary restrictions. Boarders designs premium, standalone Vegan, Vegetarian, and Gluten-Free (GF) destinations directly into the menu layout rather than handling them as an afterthought. Because elements are delivered completely deconstructed into isolated board wells, cross-contamination risks are mitigated seamlessly at the line level.

4. High-Margin Beverage Attach Strategies

Beverage programs maintain the strongest profit yields in the industry. Boarders drives average check values up by directly embedding curated, high-theatric craft cocktail pairings underneath each food menu item, removing friction from the upsell process[cite: 1].

  • Thermal Retention: Wooden layout frames will hold flush, custom-insulated wells engineered to house pre-heated cast-iron inserts straight from the line, keeping main features hot without wilting garnishes[cite: 1].
